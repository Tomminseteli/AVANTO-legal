Privacy Policy for AVΛNTO

Last Updated: March 2026

Selander Creative ("we", "us", or "our") operates the AVΛNTO mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use the App, why it is processed, and the choices available to you.

Key Points:
  • Core session data is stored locally on your device.
  • Optional features (HealthKit, Location, Notifications) require your permission.
  • Analytics and crash reporting are used only if you explicitly grant consent.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

1) Data We Process

A. Data stored locally on your device

The App stores the following locally for functionality:

  • Session date and time
  • Session duration
  • Water or air temperature
  • Session type/category
  • Notes and observations
  • Mood/symptom inputs
  • App settings and preferences
  • Notification settings

B. Optional location data (permission-based)

If you grant location permission, the App may store location coordinates and user-defined location names for session context and maps.

C. Optional Apple Health data (permission-based)

If you grant HealthKit permission, the App may:

  • Read selected health metrics (for example, heart rate)
  • Write selected workout/session records to Apple Health

D. Analytics and crash diagnostics (consent-based)

If you grant analytics consent, we may process technical and usage telemetry, such as:

  • App events (for example, screen views, flow completion events)
  • Device/app metadata (app version, OS version, device class)
  • Crash diagnostics and non-fatal error reports

This processing is used to improve reliability and product quality.

E. Data we do not intentionally collect as direct identifiers

We do not intentionally collect your name, email address, or payment card details through normal in-app use. If you contact support by email, we will receive the information you include in that message.

2) Purposes and Legal Bases (EU/EEA)

Where GDPR applies, we rely on:

  • Performance of contract: providing core app features
  • Consent: analytics/crash reporting, HealthKit access, location access, notifications
  • Legal obligations / legitimate interests: compliance, fraud/security prevention, and service integrity where applicable

You can withdraw consent at any time from app/device settings.

3) Third-Party Services and Processors

Depending on your choices, the App may rely on:

  • Apple (App Store, StoreKit subscriptions, HealthKit, iCloud backup, device notification/location frameworks)
  • Mixpanel (analytics, only with consent)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (crash diagnostics, only with consent)

These providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

4) Payments and Subscriptions

Payments are processed by Apple through your Apple ID. We do not receive or store your full payment card data. We may receive limited subscription status information (for example active/inactive tier state) to enable Pro features.

5) Data Sharing and Sale

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not use your data for third-party advertising profiling.
  • We share data with processors only as needed to provide app functionality or consented diagnostics/analytics.

6) Storage, Retention, and Deletion

  • Core app data remains on-device until you delete it in-app or uninstall the App.
  • If iCloud Backup is enabled on your device, app data may be included in Apple-managed backups.
  • Analytics/crash data sent to Mixpanel/Crashlytics is retained according to configured retention settings and provider policies.
  • You may request assistance regarding deletion requests by contacting us.

Effect of withdrawing consent

Withdrawing consent stops future analytics/crash collection from the app. It may not automatically remove data already processed before withdrawal; deletion is subject to provider capabilities and legal requirements.

7) International Transfers

Where third-party providers process data outside your country, transfers are governed by provider transfer mechanisms and applicable law (for example, standard contractual safeguards where required).

8) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

9) Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access information about processing
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Permission controls (iOS)

  • Location: Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > AVΛNTO
  • Health: Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices > AVΛNTO
  • Notifications: Settings > Notifications > AVΛNTO

10) Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 18+. We do not knowingly target or collect personal data from children for this service context.

11) Health and Safety Disclaimer

AVΛNTO is a tracking and wellness support tool, not a medical device. It does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or emergency monitoring. Consult a qualified healthcare professional before beginning or changing cold-exposure practices.

12) Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are material, we will update the "Last Updated" date and may provide additional notice in-app.

We aim to respond within a reasonable time.
