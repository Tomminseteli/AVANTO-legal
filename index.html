Privacy Policy for AVΛNTO

Last Updated: February 26, 2026

Introduction

Selander Creative ("we," "us," or "our") operates the AVΛNTO mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

Key Points:
  • All your data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not collect or transmit your data to our servers
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties
  • You have full control over your data

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

Cold Exposure Session Data:

  • Date and time of sessions
  • Duration of exposure
  • Water or air temperature
  • Location coordinates (if you grant permission)
  • Personal notes and observations
  • Session type and category

Location Data:

  • GPS coordinates of cold exposure spots
  • Custom names for locations
  • Location history for your sessions

App Settings and Preferences:

  • Your app configuration choices
  • Display preferences
  • Notification settings

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your name or personal identification
  • Email addresses or contact information (unless you contact us for support)
  • Payment information (handled entirely by Apple)
  • Your session data or health metrics
  • Your location history
  • Any personal information

How We Use Your Information

Local Processing Only

All data collected by the App is:

  • Stored exclusively on your device
  • Used only to provide the App's functionality
  • Never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Under your complete control

Purpose of Data Use

Your data is used solely to:

  • Display your cold exposure history and statistics
  • Map your favorite cold exposure locations
  • Track your progress over time
  • Sync between your iPhone and Apple Watch (via Apple's systems)
  • Provide you with insights about your cold exposure practice

Apple Health (HealthKit) Integration

What We Access

If you grant permission, the App may access Apple Health (HealthKit) to:

  • Read health metrics relevant to cold exposure (such as heart rate, if available)
  • Write your cold exposure session data to Apple Health

How We Use HealthKit Data

  • HealthKit data is used ONLY within the App for displaying your health metrics
  • We will NEVER share HealthKit data with third parties
  • We will NEVER use HealthKit data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • HealthKit data remains on your device and is managed by Apple's Health app

Your Control

  • You can grant or revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in iOS Settings > Health > Apps
  • Revoking HealthKit access does not delete the App or your session data
  • The App will continue to function without HealthKit access, but some features may be limited

Compliance

Our use of HealthKit data complies with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines and HealthKit requirements.

Location Services

Why We Use Location

The App uses your device's location services to:

  • Automatically record the location of your cold exposure sessions
  • Map and remember your favorite cold spots
  • Display your session history on a map

How Location Data is Handled

  • Location data is collected only when you use the App
  • Location coordinates are stored locally on your device
  • We do not transmit location data to our servers
  • Location data is not shared with third parties

Your Control

  • You can grant or revoke location permission at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > AVΛNTO
  • You can choose "Never," "Ask Next Time," or "While Using the App"
  • If you deny location access, the App will still function, but location-based features will not be available

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All data is stored in the App's secure local database on your device
  • Data is protected by your device's built-in security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • We do not have access to your device or your data

iCloud Backup

  • If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device, your App data may be included in your iCloud backup
  • This is managed entirely by Apple's iCloud service
  • We do not control or have access to your iCloud backups
  • You can disable iCloud backup for the App in iOS Settings

No Cloud Servers

  • We do not operate cloud servers for the App
  • We do not store your data on any remote servers
  • All data remains on your device

Data Sharing and Third Parties

No Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Share your personal data with third parties
  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Transmit your data to our servers

Apple's Role

The only third party involved with your data is Apple:

  • App Store and Subscriptions: Apple processes subscription payments through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.
  • iCloud Backup: If enabled, Apple may backup your App data as part of your iCloud backup.
  • Apple Health: If you grant permission, the App can read from and write to Apple Health, which is managed by Apple.

Subscription and Payment Information

Payment Processing

  • All subscription payments are processed by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not collect or store your payment information
  • Apple handles all billing, receipts, and refunds
  • Your subscription status is managed through your Apple ID

Subscription Data

We do not have access to:

  • Your credit card or payment details
  • Your full purchase history
  • Your personal billing information

Apple may share with us:

  • Anonymous subscription status (active/inactive)
  • Subscription tier (monthly/yearly)
  • This information is used solely to enable Premium features

Your Rights and Choices

Access Your Data

All your data is stored locally on your device and is accessible to you at any time through the App.

Delete Your Data

You can delete your data by:

  • Deleting individual sessions within the App
  • Uninstalling the App from your device
  • This will permanently delete all locally stored data

Export Your Data

The App allows you to export your session data (Premium feature) for your personal use.

Manage Permissions

You can control:

  • Location access: iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services
  • HealthKit access: iOS Settings > Health > Apps > AVΛNTO
  • Notifications: iOS Settings > Notifications > AVΛNTO

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Cold exposure activities carry health risks and should only be undertaken by adults or with proper adult supervision and medical guidance.

If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us at avantoapplication@gmail.com.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

No Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics services. We do not track your usage of the App or collect analytics data about your behavior.

Crash Reports

If the App crashes, Apple's operating system may generate a crash report. These reports:

  • Are managed by Apple
  • May be shared with us through Apple's App Store Connect
  • Contain technical information about the crash (not your personal data)
  • Help us fix bugs and improve the App

You can disable sharing crash reports in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements.

International Data Transfers

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there are no international data transfers. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it or it's included in your iCloud backup.
