Privacy Policy for AVΛNTO

Last Updated: March 2026

Introduction

Selander Creative ("we," "us," or "our") operates the AVΛNTO mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

Key Points:
  • Your cold exposure session data is stored locally on your device only
  • We collect only the account information needed to provide the service
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties
  • Marketing emails are sent only with your explicit opt-in consent
  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time

User Accounts

Account Information We Collect

When you register, we collect your email address and optionally your display name to personalise your experience. If you use Sign in with Apple, Apple may provide a private relay email address — we store only what Apple shares with us and never store your Apple ID password or credentials.

How Account Data is Stored

Your account information (email address, display name, subscription status, and marketing consent) is stored on Supabase (supabase.com), hosted on Amazon Web Services infrastructure in the EU West region (Ireland). Supabase acts as a data processor on our behalf and processes data in accordance with GDPR. Your cold exposure session data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to Supabase or any other server.

Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete Account within the app. Deletion removes your profile, email address, and login credentials from our servers permanently. This action is irreversible. All data is fully removed within 30 days of the request.

Marketing Communications

We will only send you marketing emails if you explicitly opt in during registration or in Settings → News & updates. You can withdraw your consent at any time by toggling off "News & updates" in the app Settings, or by unsubscribing from any marketing email we send. Withdrawing consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before withdrawal.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

Cold Exposure Session Data:

  • Date and time of sessions
  • Duration of exposure
  • Water or air temperature
  • Location coordinates (if you grant permission)
  • Personal notes and observations
  • Session type and category

Location Data:

  • GPS coordinates of cold exposure spots
  • Custom names for locations
  • Location history for your sessions

App Settings and Preferences:

  • Your app configuration choices
  • Display preferences
  • Notification settings

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your session data or health metrics (stored on device only)
  • Payment information (handled entirely by Apple)
  • Your location history (stored on device only)

How We Use Your Information

Account Information

We use your account information to provide and maintain your account, authenticate you securely, allow you to sign in across reinstalls, and send transactional messages (account confirmation, password reset). If you have opted in, we also use your email to send occasional updates about new features and improvements.

Session Data (Local Only)

Your session data is used solely to:

  • Display your cold exposure history and statistics
  • Map your favourite cold exposure locations
  • Track your progress over time
  • Sync between your iPhone and Apple Watch (via Apple's systems)
  • Provide you with insights about your cold exposure practice

Apple Health (HealthKit) Integration

What We Access

If you grant permission, the App may access Apple Health (HealthKit) to:

  • Read health metrics relevant to cold exposure (such as heart rate)
  • Write your cold exposure session data to Apple Health

How We Use HealthKit Data

  • HealthKit data is used ONLY within the App for displaying your health metrics
  • We will NEVER share HealthKit data with third parties
  • We will NEVER use HealthKit data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • HealthKit data remains on your device and is managed by Apple's Health app

Your Control

  • You can grant or revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in iOS Settings > Health > Apps
  • Revoking HealthKit access does not delete the App or your session data
  • The App will continue to function without HealthKit access, but some features may be limited

Location Services

Why We Use Location

The App uses your device's location services to:

  • Automatically record the location of your cold exposure sessions
  • Map and remember your favourite cold spots
  • Display your session history on a map

How Location Data is Handled

  • Location data is collected only when you use the App
  • Location coordinates are stored locally on your device
  • We do not transmit location data to our servers
  • Location data is not shared with third parties

Your Control

  • You can grant or revoke location permission at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > AVΛNTO
  • If you deny location access, the App will still function, but location-based features will not be available

Data Storage and Security

Account Data (Supabase)

Your account information is stored securely on Supabase servers in the EU West (Ireland) region. Supabase uses industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. We do not store payment information or session data server-side.

Local Session Data

  • All session data is stored in the App's secure local database on your device
  • Data is protected by your device's built-in security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • We do not have access to your locally stored session data

iCloud Backup

  • If you have iCloud Backup enabled, your local App data may be included in your iCloud backup
  • This is managed entirely by Apple's iCloud service
  • We do not control or have access to your iCloud backups

Data Sharing and Third Parties

No Data Selling

We do not sell your data to anyone, use your data for advertising, or share your personal data with third parties for their own commercial purposes.

Service Providers

Supabase: Stores your account information as described above. Acts as a data processor under GDPR.

Apple: Processes subscription payments through the App Store. Manages iCloud backups if enabled. Manages HealthKit data if permission is granted. Governed by Apple's privacy policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Subscription and Payment Information

Payment Processing

  • All subscription payments are processed by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not collect or store your payment information
  • Apple handles all billing, receipts, and refunds
  • Your subscription status is managed through your Apple ID

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Correction

Your session data is accessible at any time through the App. You can update your display name in Settings.

Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account from Settings → Delete Account. This removes all account data from our servers within 30 days. Your locally stored session data is removed when you delete the App from your device.

Export Your Data

The App allows you to export your session data (Pro feature) for your personal use.

Manage Permissions

  • Location access: iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services
  • HealthKit access: iOS Settings > Health > Apps > AVΛNTO
  • Marketing emails: Settings → News & updates (toggle off at any time)

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Cold exposure activities carry health risks and should only be undertaken by adults or with proper adult supervision and medical guidance.

If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us at avantoapplication@gmail.com.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

No Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics services. We do not track your usage of the App or collect analytics data about your behaviour.

Crash Reports

If the App crashes, Apple's operating system may generate a crash report. These reports are managed by Apple, may be shared with us through Apple's App Store Connect, and contain only technical information about the crash — not your personal data.

European Union (EU) and GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data (use Settings → Delete Account)
  • Restriction — restrict certain processing
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format (use the export feature)
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw marketing consent at any time

To exercise these rights, use the account management features in the app or contact us at avantoapplication@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In Finland, this is the Office of the Data Protection Ombudsman (tietosuoja.fi).

Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law. Session data stored locally on your device is retained until you delete the App or manually remove sessions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top and notify you through the App. Your continued use of the App after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

Health and Safety Disclaimer

This App is for tracking purposes only and does not provide medical advice. The data collected and displayed by the App should not be used for medical diagnosis or treatment. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider before beginning cold exposure activities.
